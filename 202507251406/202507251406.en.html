Introduction

The game revolves around the protagonist Beni, a father struggling to support his family. However, he falls into the predicament of gambling and illegal loans, putting the entire family on the verge of breaking apart. Players will experience the sufferings of Beni and his family through the game, and deeply feel the devastating impact of gambling on families.

Story-Driven Gameplay

The game adopts a story-centered gameplay. Every decision made by the player in the game will affect the development of the plot and the fate of the characters. This gameplay allows players to truly immerse themselves in Beni's story, feel the various dilemmas and choices he faces, and enhances the sense of immersion and emotional resonance of the game.

Multiple Endings Design

The game features six different endings, depending on the player's choices during the game. Different endings show various possible consequences of gambling problems. In some endings, Beni ultimately cannot withstand the pressure and chooses to commit suicide; in others, he takes extreme measures to repay debts, such as selling his organs. These endings profoundly reflect the serious harm of gambling to individuals and families, allowing players to have a deeper thinking about gambling problems after the game ends.

Emotionally Intense Narrative Style

The game uses emotionally intense narrative techniques to vividly depict the pain, struggle and despair of Beni and his family when facing the gambling crisis. Through delicate emotional expressions, players can more deeply understand the seriousness of gambling problems and the huge damage they cause to family and personal life.

Presentation of Sensitive Themes

It unreservedly presents sensitive themes such as domestic violence, financial collapse, and family trauma. Through intuitive pictures and plot settings, players can understand that gambling problems are not just personal economic losses, but also trigger a series of serious social and family problems, further emphasizing the educational significance of the game.

Game Significance

Educational and Warning Role

Through vivid game plots and multiple endings design, it intuitively shows players the dangers of gambling, playing a good educational and warning role. While entertaining, players can deeply realize the possible serious consequences of gambling, thus staying away from gambling.

Gameplay

1. After clicking New Game, there will be a prompt about sensitive content in the game and a skill test. For the sensitive content prompt, you must choose according to your physical and mental conditions. For the skill test, you need to keep clicking the Tap button until the progress bar is full, then click Continue to start the game.

2. After entering the game, the protagonist sits on a chair. The protagonist can pick up the cigarette or TV remote control on the table. After picking up the TV remote control, aim it at the TV to change channels. During this period, the protagonist cannot move until the wife comes to interact with you, and then the story unfolds.

3. If you feel the sensitivity is too fast, click the pause button in the upper right corner to adjust the Sensitivity option. Fov Camera is the perspective option; the higher it is, the wider the perspective. After adjustment, click Back to the game below to continue the game. If you want to quit the game, click pause and then click Quit Game in the lower right corner.

Endings Overview

Ending 1 Conclusion: All bitter news and stories about gambling ruining lives initially start with "just playing for fun" (Best ending)

Ending 2 Conclusion: The second you realize gambling is hurting you is the golden opportunity to save yourself (Good ending)
